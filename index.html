PhysEd is a Simple, Fun and Easy to Use Service if students want to self-learn the interactive way!!


PhysEd is a new, enjoyable, simple way to teach yourself at your own pace about Physics and Maths in an interactive, intriguing and practical way, the experiments are related to Applied Maths and Physics which are ongoing in everyday life! You can also compete with your friends on Leaderboards or by creating your own challenges! You can use interactive animations, diagrams and graphics to create your own, personal experiments assited by guides! You can Subscribe for more content and no Ads or complete the Daily Goals to unlock more content!!
